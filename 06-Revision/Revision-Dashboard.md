## Revision Strategy

> [!tip] Effective Revision Cycle
> 1. **Day 1:** Read unit notes and highlight key points
> 2. **Day 3:** Review without notes, write from memory
> 3. **Day 7:** Solve past questions on the unit
> 4. **Day 14:** Quick recall review (5 minutes per topic)
> 5. **Pre-exam:** Only revision summary callouts
