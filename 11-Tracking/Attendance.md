## Attendance Alert

> [!caution] Dangerously Low Attendance
> If any subject falls below **75%**, you CANNOT appear for final exams.
> Contact the Subject Teacher immediately for medical leave consideration.

**Calculation example:**
- If total lectures = 60
- Required attendance = 60 × 0.75 = **45 lectures minimum**
- If you miss 15+ lectures, you are in danger zone!
