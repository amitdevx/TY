# Agile & Scrum Interview Preparation

1. **What is the difference between Agile and Waterfall?**
   Agile is iterative and flexible. Waterfall is linear and rigid.
2. **What are the roles in Scrum?**
   Product Owner, Scrum Master, and Developers.
3. **What is velocity in Agile?**
   The amount of work (in story points) a team can complete in a single sprint.
4. **Explain the difference between Definition of Ready (DoR) and Definition of Done (DoD).**
   DoR: Criteria a user story must meet before it can be accepted into a sprint. DoD: Criteria a story must meet to be considered complete and releasable.
5. **What happens in a Daily Scrum?**
   15-minute timeboxed event for Developers. What did I do yesterday? What will I do today? Any impediments?
