# Agile Processes - Important Questions

> [!summary] About
> Highly probable exam questions for CS-371 (University Exam: 25 Marks). 

---

## Short Answer Questions (2-3 Marks)

1. State the 4 core values of the Agile Manifesto.
2. Define 'Increment' in the context of Scrum.
3. What is the time-box for a Sprint and a Daily Scrum?
4. Differentiate between Epic and User Story.
5. What are the three roles defined in the Scrum Framework?
6. Define Velocity in Agile.
7. What is the purpose of the Sprint Retrospective?
8. State any two principles of Lean Software Development.
9. What is a WIP Limit in Kanban?
10. Define Test-Driven Development (TDD).

---

## Medium Answer Questions (5 Marks)

11. Explain the differences between traditional Waterfall model and Agile methodology.
12. Detail the responsibilities of a Scrum Master. How does it differ from a traditional Project Manager?
13. Describe the Product Backlog and Sprint Backlog. How are they managed?
14. Explain the Kanban framework. How does it differ from Scrum?
15. What is Planning Poker? Explain the steps involved in estimating a user story using Planning Poker.
16. Describe the CI/CD pipeline and its importance in Agile.
17. Explain the RED-GREEN-REFACTOR cycle of Test-Driven Development.

---

## Long Answer / Scenario Questions (Not typical for 25-mark paper, but good for understanding)

18. **Scenario:** Your Development Team consistently fails to complete all the items in the Sprint Backlog by the end of the Sprint. 
    - Identify possible reasons for this failure.
    - As a Scrum Master, what steps would you take during the Sprint Retrospective to resolve this?

19. Explain the complete lifecycle of a User Story from its creation in the Product Backlog to it becoming part of a "Done" Increment. Mention the relevant Scrum events.
