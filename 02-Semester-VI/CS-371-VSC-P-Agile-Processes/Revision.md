# CS-371 Quick Revision

- **Agile Manifesto**: Individuals/interactions over processes/tools; Working software over documentation; Customer collab over contract neg; Responding to change over following plan.
- **Scrum Roles**: Product Owner (maximizes value), Scrum Master (facilitates), Developers.
- **Scrum Events**: Sprint (1-4 weeks), Planning, Daily Scrum (15 min), Review, Retrospective.
- **User Stories**: "As a [role], I want [feature] so that [benefit]".
- **Estimation**: Planning Poker, Story Points (Fibonacci).
- **Prioritization**: MoSCoW (Must, Should, Could, Won't).
- **Metrics**: Velocity (work done per sprint), Burndown Chart (work remaining over time).
