## 5. Viva Questions
1. **What is the goal of Linear Regression?**
   - To find the optimal line of best fit that minimizes the sum of squared residuals between predicted and actual values.
2. **What does the R-squared score indicate?**
   - It indicates the goodness of fit of the model. An R-squared of 1 implies the model perfectly predicts the target variable.
3. **Why do we split data into training and testing sets?**
   - To evaluate the model's performance on unseen data, ensuring the model generalizes well and does not just overfit the training data.
4. **What is Mean Squared Error (MSE)?**
   - It is the average of the squared differences between the predicted and actual values. Lower MSE implies better accuracy.
5. **What is the difference between Simple and Multiple Linear Regression?**
   - Simple uses one independent variable, while multiple uses two or more independent variables to predict the target.
