## 5. Viva Questions
1. **What is the primary purpose of EDA?**
   - To understand the data structure, discover underlying patterns, detect outliers, and test underlying assumptions before modeling.
2. **What does a correlation coefficient of 0.9 signify?**
   - It signifies a very strong positive linear relationship between two variables.
3. **What is a Pair Plot?**
   - A grid of scatter plots visualizing all pairwise relationships of numerical features, often color-coded by a categorical target variable.
4. **Why do we use heatmaps in EDA?**
   - Heatmaps visually present the intensity of data. They are especially useful for quickly identifying highly correlated features in a correlation matrix.
5. **What function provides summary statistics for numerical columns in a Pandas DataFrame?**
   - `df.describe()`
