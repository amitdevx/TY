# Viva Questions for Assignment 3: Backlog Prioritization and Release Planning

1. Explain the MoSCoW prioritization technique.
2. What are Story Points, and why do agile teams use them instead of hours?
3. Define "Team Velocity" and explain how it is calculated.
4. Who has the final say on the priority of items in the Product Backlog?
5. How do you handle a situation where a "Must Have" feature is estimated to take more points than the team's velocity for a single sprint?
6. What is a spike, and when should it be added to the backlog?
7. Why is it dangerous to treat estimates as strict deadlines?
8. How frequently should the Product Backlog be groomed (refined)?
