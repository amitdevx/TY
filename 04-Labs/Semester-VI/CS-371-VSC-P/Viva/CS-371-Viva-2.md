# Viva Questions for Assignment 2: User Stories and Acceptance Criteria

1. What are the three components of a standard User Story?
2. What does the acronym INVEST stand for in the context of User Stories?
3. Why is it important to write User Stories from the user's perspective rather than the developer's?
4. What is the difference between Acceptance Criteria and a Definition of Done (DoD)?
5. How detailed should Acceptance Criteria be?
6. Can a User Story have no Acceptance Criteria? Why or why not?
7. Who is typically responsible for writing User Stories?
8. Give an example of a poorly written user story and explain why it is bad.
